    The new Peer Tutoring Program

    Peer tutoring now happens in tutors’ dorm lounges—there may be a tutor available right in your dorm!

    Use the table on the left to find tutors closest to your dorm.

    You have to make appointments with your tutor via email/chat before each visit!

    • 1. Enter your request

      Select your Subject, Dorm, and Date to find matching tutors.  There is no Search button as the list updates automatically as you select.

    • 2. Browse the tutor result list

      Tutors that matches your subject are shown in the result list.  They are sorted based on distance to your dorm.

    • 3. Contact a tutor

      Click the link next to your tutor’s name to send an email.  Contact your tutor via email or Peddie Email chat at least a few hours before study hall.  The tutors will be waiting for you in dorm lounges or their rooms on duty days.

    • Tutors are generally available 8–10 PM on their duty days.  We are serious about duty days—if a tutor is not available on a certain day, do not contact him/her.
